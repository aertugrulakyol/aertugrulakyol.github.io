Crypto Mobile App

In this case study, I aim to develop a Crypto Wallet app that caters specifically to the needs of cryptocurrency enthusiasts, with a primary focus on crypto assets, decentralized finance (DeFi), and non-fungible tokens (NFTs). The project will revolve around designing and developing a comprehensive mobile application that offers a seamless and secure experience for managing and utilizing digital assets in the crypto ecosystem.

Design

Login Screen

The app's landing screen instructs you to either create a new wallet or log in with an existing one. In terms of user experience, the primary button to create a new electronic wallet is much better because users who already have one know how to do it and don't need any help as opposed to new users.

Home Page

The user's profile, search function, notifications, total funds, and comparison to last week are all accessible from the home page. With the send, receive, swap, and buy buttons, users can complete quick transactions. They can also view their assets as crypto nfts or defi.

Transactions

The user can easily filter it using a button while viewing all of the transactions they have made on this screen. To help the user understand, transactions are broken down by date and categorized by icons.

Explore

From this screen, users can explore popular cryptocurrencies, nfts collections, gaming, defi assets, and much more. A search bar is also available at the top if users want to look for something in particular.

Buy

In the buy screens, users can see the asset's current market value as well as its highest and lowest values. They can also narrow or widen the graph based on a span of time, such as an hour, day, week, month, or year. Along with the buy cta, the user can also check the asset's current balance and transaction history to direct them to the following step of the purchasing process.

Info

The asset's current value, as well as its highest and lowest values, are displayed on the info screen. The user can easily take action with the buttons for receive, send, swap, and buy.

Settings

The user can change a variety of settings on this page, including dark mode, language preferences, currency selections, and 2FA authentications. Users can navigate the settings screen much more easily when the settings are categorized.

Search & Notifications

Users can access any assets by using the search function, and the auto-complete search bar and suggestion feature will cut down on the amount of time they have to spend typing. The user can receive various alerts, such as news, price alerts, volatility alerts, and others, at the notifications screen. A user can quickly swipe to delete a notification or mark all of them as read by pressing a single button.
