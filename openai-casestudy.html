Improving Navigation & Offline Access on OpenAI

I wanted to enhance the usability of OpenAI as it is a relatively new web application. In daily use, the absence of one or two features can be frustrating for users. This presents a good opportunity for me to study the shipped product. Therefore, I experimented on the live project to see how it goes.

Problem

Having multiple answers displayed at once can take up a lot of space on the screen and make it difficult to locate specific answers or questions.

Solution

Implement a collapsible answers section on OpenAI, allowing users to easily view one answer at a time and improve their ability to locate specific answers or questions.

I'm introducing a new feature to make it easier for you to navigate through OpenAI answers. When you hover over a question, you'll see a button appear next to the edit icon. If you click on it, it will collapse the answers, so you can see the question you're looking for right next to the answer it belongs to, making it easier for you to find the information you need.

Problem

High demand on OpenAI systems causes previous conversations to be temporarily unavailable.

Solution

Allow users the option to download previous conversations as a PDF, save them to Google Drive, or send them via email.

I've added a button right next to the edit option in the sidebar for the selected title. When you click on it, you'll see options appear such as sending it to Google Drive, downloading it as a PDF document, or sending it to an email. So you can easily access your conversation even if you're offline!

What About Mobile?

I've made it even easier for you to navigate through the answers on mobile. Now, you can simply tap on the title and it will collapse the answers. And don't worry, the edit button is still easily accessible for you to use. I've made it just as easy for you to download your conversations on mobile as it is on desktop. Now, whether you're on your phone or computer, you can easily save or share your conversation with just a tap or a click.
