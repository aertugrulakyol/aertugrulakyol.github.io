I bring a unique blend of skills and experience to my role as a UX/UI Designer. With 10 years of bartending experience, I have developed a deep understanding of customer needs and how to meet them, which is a valuable asset in designing user-centered experiences.

I am eager to bring my unique background and perspective to the table and help create exceptional user experiences. Let's work together to make something truly remarkable.
