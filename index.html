About

My name is Ertugrul and I am a UX designer based in Ankara, Turkey. I have always been passionate about creating intuitive and user-friendly designs that solve real problems for people.

I have a degree in Internet & Network Technologies and Google UX Design certificate. I specialize in designing for web and mobile platforms, and I am skilled in using tools such as Figma, Adobe XD, Photoshop and Illustrator.
