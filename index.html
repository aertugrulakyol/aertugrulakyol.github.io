About

I bring a unique blend of skills and experience to my role as a UX/UI Designer. With 10 years of bartending experience, I have developed a deep understanding of customer needs and how to meet them, which is a valuable asset in designing user-centered experiences.

My journey into the field of UX/UI design began when the pandemic hit and I decided to pursue a new career path. As I studied programming, I discovered my passion for UX design and its potential to create meaningful experiences for users. In my free time, I love to play video games and explore new tastes, and I also stay up-to-date on the latest design trends.

I am a recent graduate of Ankara University with a degree in Internet and Network Technologies, and have further honed my skills through Google's UX Design course. I have expertise in Figma and the Creative Cloud suite of applications, as well as CSS, HTML, and JavaScript.

I am eager to bring my unique background and perspective to the table and help create exceptional user experiences. Let's work together to make something truly remarkable."
