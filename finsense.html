Startup Landing Page

With the introduction of AI, startups have created numerous AI-powered tools and have adopted specific trends for their landing pages. Since no-code tools have grown in power year after year, designers have begun publishing their work online. I want to create a current, hip startup template for this project, which will soon have its development on Webflow completed.

ROLE

Product Designer, No-Code Developer

DURATION

4 Weeks

PROCESS

Research

Ideation

Wireframe

Design

Hero

The CTA is clearly placed in the middle of the centered hero page to make it easy for users to interact with it, and the product sample is placed slightly off-center to encourage users to scroll.

Features

After reading the hero section, users can interact with features to learn more about the products. To prevent the user's cognitive load from becoming overloading, it supports with icons and shorter phrases.

Pricing

User can easily choose a specific plan by viewing the pricing and available plans themselves. One option can be highlighted with encouraging text, a more colorful design, and the primary CTA.

Testimonials

Comments from customers aid in understanding the product's benefits and drawbacks. Customers can gain trust in the product and make simple decisions in this way.

FQA

Users can find answers to the most frequent questions that can benefit both parties in frequently asked questions. Users receive their answers much more quickly and the company saves manpower on support.

Footer

There is a sitemap and a place to sign up for a newsletter in the footer. To give users one final choice, the footer also has a CTA similar to the one in the hero section.That way the user won't have to scroll all the way to the top to see the CTA.
