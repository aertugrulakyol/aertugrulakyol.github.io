About Me

I've spent my entire life looking for a way to express my creativity, and I've finally come to the conclusion that product design is the perfect fit for me. I wanted to learn programming before starting this journey, and during the pandemic, I attended Ankara University's department of internet and network technologies, where I graduated with highest grades. As a product designer now, this has actually given me skills that are extremely valuable, like programming language knowledge and improved engineering communication.

I started working as a freelancer as soon as I received my college degree. The objective was to create the website for a business called Hidrojeo. However, after the project was complete, regardless of how happy the client was, I realized there was a lot I could do better, so I began my research. I then learned about the idea of user experience and signed up for the Google UX Design course. After taking this course and expanding my knowledge of user experience, I learned the principles of visual design and picked up skills in user interface, illustration, and photoshop.

Due to my interest and enthusiasm in video games, I looked for a freelance position to create game interfaces and collaborated with an independent game producer who created the title Balqabi.

At Kiraz.co, I'm currently continuing my journey as a product designer in the areas of SaaS and e-commerce site design.

I'm excited to use this rich and varied experience to collaborate with you on my current and upcoming projects!
