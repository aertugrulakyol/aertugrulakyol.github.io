Game UI

I successfully designed a game UI as a freelance job for an indie game developer. The game, called Balqabi, is a futuristic puzzle game that revolves around the concept of artificial intelligence taking over the world. The client specifically requested a futuristic and minimalistic design, and I delivered a visually captivating interface that met their requirements. The client expressed great satisfaction with the design, which further highlights my ability to understand and fulfill the unique preferences of my clients.

Main Menu

The main menu has options for continue, new game, load game, options, accessibility, and quit, which is pretty conventional for game UI.

Options

To avoid confusion, I've divided options into categories for gameplay, graphics, audio controls, and accessibility. In options, users can adjust a variety of settings. Additionally, I divided the screen into two columns so that when a user hovers over an option, an explanation with images and text is displayed.

Pause Menu & Hud

At the pause menu I decided to use a blurred background but left the user with a small hint as to when the game was paused. Additionally, I try to keep the hud as simple as possible to meet client expectations.
