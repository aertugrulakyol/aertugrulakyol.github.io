SaaS Application

In this conceptual project, I aim to develop a Customer Relationship Management (CRM) web app that offers a fully customizable dashboard and integrates essential functions such as in-app email, calendar, and task planning. This case study will explore the design and development of a comprehensive CRM solution tailored to meet the diverse needs of businesses. By leveraging user-centric design principles and advanced technology, the goal is to create a seamless and intuitive platform that enhances sales and customer management processes.

Design

Login Screen

With the logo mark and illustration, I improved the login screen's sense of brand identity. With the remember me and forgot password options, I helped the user navigate the site more easily.

Dashboard

The user can add customizable widgets to the dashboard while the screen is empty. With examples and supporting texts, I imply that, and for this I pointed them toward the "create new" CTA. Additionally, I elevated and shadowed the settings menu and notifications menu to make them stand out.

Sales

I made it easier for users to plan by including a calendar and a to-do list for each day. Users have access to a list that allows them to follow the current state of sales at all times.

Leads

Users have the ability to add new leads, export all leads, and group existing leads by status in leads.

Contacts

Users can access contacts and view information about them on the contacts page, including tags and email and phone number status notifications.

Mails

Users can access their emails more easily through the application by integrating them, and they can complete all of their email tasks without ever leaving it.

Settings

Users have access to numerous processes' settings, including integration of third-party applications and profile settings.

Dark Mode

Users can switch between light and dark theme as they wish.
