Asist Accounting

Asist is an accounting firm based in Ankara, Turkey, with 30 years of experience in the field. They wanted to increase their recognition on the web to attract new clients and approached me to design and code a static website from scratch.

The client requested a minimalist and elegant website that also stands out from others. That's why I chose a minimalist approach with neumorphic elements. The project took approximately a month, which exceeded the client's expectations.
